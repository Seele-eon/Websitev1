What For?

NFTs? Really?

Always tied to higher work. Expands on the main piece.

The NFTs embody the theme of the “L” series work to which they belong. As such, the NFTs might expand on the themes of the main work. After all, beauty is in the eye of the beholder.

To clarify, everything that needs to be shown is within the main work. Look closely... or perhaps not too closely.

Not mass-produced. But made with love.

A set of "L" Originals are only minted with the release of a main work in the "L" series. Even if I collaborate with other people in any future work from the "L" series, every "L" Original is produced by me.

A win-win for interested parties

Seele-eon gets to make a living through art.

NFT purchaser gets the opportunity to own a unique piece of art, which stems from a main work of the "L" Series.

The audience gets NFT-funded downloads, which allow firstcomers to experience the next work in the “L” series without charge.
